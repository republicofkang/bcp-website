Southwind Apartments

It is a 1992 brick 144-unit property (12 buildings) that is about a 24-minute drive from Downtown Cincinnati with fortune 500 companies and an influx of new businesses due to its pro-business laws and booming economy. The property is right next to a grocery and other essentials, 5 miles away from a mall and medical facility with 10K+ high-paying jobs. Another older property without upgrades is renting at $300/unit higher, and it's only 4 minutes away. This will be a value add deal with early distribution. The business plan is to do rehab on all units and sell in 3 years.

Why did you choose Cincinnati,OH market?

The price per unit is much lower compare to other hot markets and yet the rent growth is higher. Therefore investors get better return on the investment compare to elsewhere. The population is growing, 4.2% over the past 10 years from World Population Review. Downtown Cincinnati has 7 Fortune 500 companies headquarter (Kroger, P&G, AMERICAN FINANCIAL GROUP, Fifth Third Bank, W&S, Cintas, AK steel), which means solid employment base. It's also a major transportation for Amazin and DHL, thats a strong indicator for demand.

Crime Issue? Median Income? School District?

For the crime rate, the Cincinati’s metro area is higher than the national’s average, as do all cities in United States. But this property is about 20 mins drive to the metro area and it's in a “green” area on the crime map.
The 2021 median household income for 1 mile demographics is $76,549, similar to Houston.
There are elementry school, middle school, and high school within 2 miles of this property, and it's also near four colleges: University of Cincinnati, Xavier University, UC Clermont College, Northern Kentucky University.

Market vacancy rate?

The average occupancy rate of the eight comparable apartment is 97%, very strong market.

How long do you hold the asset? Exit strategy?

We plan to hold the property three years, upgrade it and push its rent to the market rent. We will be upgrading all units which will provide the best product in the market at a very competitive price yet still provide our investor a very big profit margin. Obviously if the market is so strong, there is no reason not to consider selling it earlier than year 3. If we decide to re-fi, we will update the new MFQA at that time.

Minimum investment? Maximum investment?

The minimum is $25K and maximum is $500K. Yes, we take self-directed IRA.

What is the project return for this deal?

7-8% net return on investment (cash on cash) year 2 and year 3. Possible early distribution year 1 for about 4%. In addition, 88% of gain to passives from sale in year 3 (on top of returning 100% of original investment) or 104% of original investment returned from refinance at year 3.
